[h1]Game Adjustments[/h1]

A gameplay mechanics adjustment mod made for entertainment purposes.

[h2]NPC Defense Algorithm[/h2]

This mod changes how defense is calculated for all NPCs. When an NPC takes damage, the mod uses an IL edit to modify `NPC.HitModifiers.GetDamage`, converts the defense reduction calculated by vanilla into percentage-based damage reduction with diminishing returns, and applies it to `FinalDamage`. The original flat damage reduction is then set to 0 to prevent it from being applied twice.

[code]
Damage reduction = Vanilla defense reduction / (Defense scaling constant + Vanilla defense reduction)
Final damage multiplier = Defense scaling constant / (Defense scaling constant + Vanilla defense reduction)
[/code]

[h2]Defense Effects by Difficulty[/h2]

The defense scaling constant changes with the game difficulty. A lower constant provides greater damage reduction for the same vanilla defense reduction value.

[tr]

When the vanilla-calculated defense reduction value is 50, with no other damage modifiers:

[list]
[*]Normal Mode: 33.33% damage reduction, takes 66.67% damage
[*]Expert Mode: 40% damage reduction, takes 60% damage
[*]Master Mode: 50% damage reduction, takes 50% damage
[/list]

[h2]Features[/h2]

[list]
[*]Higher vanilla defense reduction values provide greater damage reduction to NPCs
[*]The benefit of each additional point of defense reduction gradually decreases
[*]Under normal circumstances, defense will not provide 100% damage reduction
[*]Applies to all vanilla NPCs
[*]Also applies to NPCs added by other mods
[/list]
